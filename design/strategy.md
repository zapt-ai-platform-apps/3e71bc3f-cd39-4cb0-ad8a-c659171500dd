# Design Strategy

## Core Values

- **Clarity**: Information is immediately understandable
- **Efficiency**: Tasks completed with minimal effort
- **Confidence**: Users feel in control and informed

## Design Priorities

1. **Speed of use**
2. **User confidence**
3. **Accessibility**
4. **Visual impact**
5. **Information density**

## Key Characteristics

- **Guided experience**
- **Quick scanning**
- **Consistent patterns**
